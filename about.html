About Abai

Abai Kunanbayev (1845–1904) was a Kazakh poet, composer, and philosopher. He is considered the founder of modern Kazakh literature and played an important role in the cultural development of Kazakhstan.

Important Events

  1. 1845 – Born in Semipalatinsk region
  2. 1880s – Began his literary and philosophical work
  3. 1904 – Passed away, leaving legacy

Key Contributions

  • Poetry and philosophical writings
  • Translations of world literature
  • Promotion of education and moral values
